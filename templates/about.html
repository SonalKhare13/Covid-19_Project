About Our Project

Our data provides a visualization of ICU beds in USA and covid-19 cases in the months of january to March 2020.

The map show the population, ICU beds.

The line graphs depicts covid-19 confirmed cases in the first three months of 2020 . The pie chart will depict the ration of confirmed, recoverd and death cases within a state
